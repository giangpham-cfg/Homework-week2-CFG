FAQ

Payment

One the website only payments by credit card are accepted (MasterCard, Visa and American Express) and Twint application. It is not possible to pay by invoice or Reka-Checks. Orders aren't possible by e-mail.

Age group

A Magic Pass is not required for children under 6 years of age and therefore not available: you do not have to purchase a ticket for them at our partner resorts. However, you must obtain a lift pass for them at the ticket counter. For the 2022/2023 season, children born between the years 2007 and 2016 must purchase a Child pass. People aged 16 years (year of birth is proof) and older must pay for an adult pass.

Glacier 3000 Magic Pass 2022-2023

Glacier 3000 is accessible from November 1st 2022 to April 30th 2023 with the Magic Pass.
