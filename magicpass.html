Validities, options and categories

The Magic Pass is an annual pass for anybody, which gives free access from the summer opening until the end of the following winter season in any of our partner resorts.

Length of validity 2022/2023: 1 May 2022 - 30 April 2023.

For an additional fee to the Magic Pass, discover our options

  • The Pools option gives a free access to 13 different pools and thermal baths of our resorts!

  • The option Crans Montana Aminona Pass 100 allows you to Ski 100 days on the high plateau ski area from Crans-Montana. More information here.

Magic Pass still offers you two insurance options:

  • The Pass insurance reimburses the cost of your pass in case of accidents and illness.

  • The Pass+Rescue insurance offers the added benefit of reimbursing the costs of any rescue services in the case you have an accident while skiing at a partner resort.

This service is available at a special promotional rate.
